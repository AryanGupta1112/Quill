# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus discussions on technical outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues or pull requests.
